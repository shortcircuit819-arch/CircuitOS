# CircuitOS 0.7.3.1 — Share all collections at once

A small follow-up to 0.7.3's collection packs: you can now share your **whole set of collections** as
one pack, not just one at a time.

## Share All

The Collections toolbar has a new **Share All** button. It bundles **every permanent collection** into
a single `.circuitcollection` pack — same rules as a single-collection share (it carries the flavor and
adopts the importer's theme), just all at once. Importing it creates one new profile containing all of
them.

**Event collections stay put.** Events are tied to your channel's dates, so they never travel — not in
a single share and not in Share All. (The per-collection Share button no longer appears on event
collections.)

## Notes

- Nothing in your data changes.
- Existing single-collection packs still import fine.
