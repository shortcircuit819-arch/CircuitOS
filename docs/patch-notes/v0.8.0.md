# CircuitOS 0.8.0 — Design & Identity

0.8 is a top-to-bottom visual pass. The control panel now reads as a designed product — calmer, crisper,
and consistent under any color you throw at it — and you get real control over the look: pick a theme,
pick your accent, and fine-tune from there.

## A cleaner control panel

The whole admin moved to a "hairlines over cards" look: structure comes from thin dividing lines and
subtle surfaces instead of heavy boxed-and-shadowed cards. Data rows read as clean tables, chunky pill
tags are gone (crisp small corners now), and on/off switches are simple checkboxes. Less clutter, more
signal — the same information, easier to scan.

## Themes — pick your vibe

Appearance now gives you **six designed themes** instead of a wall of color pickers:

- **Midnight** — the classic cool blue-black.
- **Slate** — neutral gray.
- **Carbon** — near-black.
- **Ember** — warm dark.
- **Grape** — deep purple.
- **Daylight** — a proper **light theme**.

Each one is a crafted set of structural colors, so the panel always looks intentional. You just pick one.

## Your accent, made safe

On top of your theme you pick a single **accent color** — your brand pop. CircuitOS keeps it legible for
you: the accent is nudged where it needs to be so text and controls stay readable, and body text always
has enough contrast on your chosen surface. Pick a wild accent and the panel still holds together.

## Design Mode

Want to go further? **Appearance → Design Mode** opens deeper controls:

- **Roundness** — dial the corner radius from sharp to soft.
- **Advanced** — override individual colors (surfaces, lines, text, status) for full control.
- **Reset** — one click back to your theme, anytime.

Design Mode edits layer *on top of* your theme, so nothing is ever permanently changed — you can always
reset cleanly. Overrides save with your profile.

## Notes

- **Nothing in your data changes.** Existing profiles keep their exact look (shown as "Custom") until you
  choose a theme. Local mode is still the default.
- Your theme also drives the OBS overlay, so the two stay in sync.
