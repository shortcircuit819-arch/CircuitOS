# CircuitOS 0.9.2 — Bot login fix

A fix for connecting a bot chat account.

## "Connect Bot Account" now opens Twitch

Clicking **Connect Bot Account** (Twitch page) previously showed a code but didn't open anything — it
looked like nothing happened. Now it opens the Twitch authorization page, just like the main-account
login, and the panel shows the code to enter.

**Important:** sign in as your **bot** account on the page that opens. If your browser is already logged
in to your main channel, click **Switch accounts** there, or use a private/incognito window — otherwise
you'd authorize your main account instead of the bot.

To help with exactly that mistake, CircuitOS now checks the result: if you accidentally authorize your
main account, it tells you clearly and asks you to try again as the bot, instead of silently connecting
the wrong account.

## Notes

- Nothing in your data changes.
- No bot connected? Chat replies post from your main account, exactly as before.
